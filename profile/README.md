## :wave: Welcome to Pro Games Studio!

> *Small team. Big ideas.*

**Pro Games Studio** is an indie game studio focused on building experiences.

We care about games that make you think: worlds that feel alive, mechanics that respect your intelligence, and stories worth remembering.

## What we build
We make 2D games.
No live service fluff, no predatory monetization.
Just games, crafted with care.

## Philosophy
We believe indie games are one of the last places where a small group of people with a clear vision can build something genuinely original.
That's the space we're trying to occupy.
