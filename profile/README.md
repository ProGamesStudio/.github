## :wave: Welcome to Pro Games Studio!

> *Small team. Big ideas.*

**Pro Games Studio** is an indie game studio focused on building experiences.

We care about games that make you think: worlds that feel alive, mechanics that respect your intelligence, and stories worth remembering.

## What we build
> We don't make just games, we make full gaming experiences.

Our games are of any type, any genre, whatever the idea calls for.

No live service fluff, no predatory monetization, no dark patterns.

We only build games **we genuinely care about**.
Every project starts as something we'd want to play ourselves, and we see it through to the end: sweating the small details, because the small details are what players actually feel.

> These are our dream games. We hope they become yours too.

## Philosophy
We believe indie games are special: one of the last corners of an industry increasingly built around monetization where players can still find something authentic.

Big studios ship fewer masterpieces.
The pressure to monetize, retain, and scale leaves little room for genuine creative risk.
We think that's a problem worth pushing back on.

Our developers are free.
No external pressure, no preset targets, no design choices made by a spreadsheet.
That freedom is where our creativity comes from.

But freedom comes with responsibility: to the player, who deserves an honest experience; to the process, which deserves to be respected regardless of the outcome; and to the idea itself, because every idea born from a human mind is worth taking seriously.

> Every game we ship carries a piece of us, and we believe that's something players can feel.
